Preeti Mahajan is a traveller, a scuba diver and a motor biker. After graduating from NIFT in 2013, Preeti started her career with warps and wefts, and textile design. From there, she transitioned fully into the design industry as a design entrepreneur with expertise in user experience, branding, brand positioning, data analysis and synthesis. With a passion for narrating chronicles through visuals for almost a decade, Preeti is currently pursuing an MDes in Digital Futures at OCAD University. Preeti is the Co-founder and Creative Director of Project 1.6, an attitude agency that works with brands in the sustainable space.

Prayag Ichangimath is a social development practitioner and design thinker originally from India. He graduated with his BE in Mechatronics Engineering from Visvesvaraya Technological University, India in 2015. He is currently based in Toronto, attending OCAD University for his MDes in Digital Futures. Over the last six years, Prayag has designed and managed developmental activities with Barefoot College in India and Africa. Being a recipient of the prestigious 3M Young Innovators Challenge Award and SBI Youth for India Fellowship, he has designed and implemented an initiative that promoted Science, Technology Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) education across 46 rural Indian schools.
